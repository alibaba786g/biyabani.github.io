Durood Hazaara/Alfa

Who ever recites this Durood in the Qabrastan [Muslim grave yard] three times, Allahu ta 'ala will take away the punishment of the grave of eighty [80] years.

Whoever recites it four times in the Qabrastan Allah Subhaana Hoo will take away the Azab ul Quboor [punishment of the grave] until the day of judgement.

Who ever recites it twenty four times Allah Subhaana Hoo will send a thousand angels who will look over, oversee and protect his parents in the grave till the Day of Judgement.
