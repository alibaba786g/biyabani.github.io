Tafsir al-Mazhari (تفسير المظهري)

By Qadi Thana'ullah Pani-pati Naqshbandi (قاضي ثناء الله پاني پتي نقشبندي)

Language: Arabic

Tafsir Mazhari is a famous Sufi tafsir of the Holy Quran written by the great Indian Hanafi scholar Qadi Thana'ullah Pani-pati, who was a Naqshbandi shaykh and khalifa of Mirza Mazhar Jan-e-Janan. He devoted the name of the Tafsir to his Shaykh Mirza Mazhar.

The Tafsir follows the Hanafi school of law and discusses Sufi concepts as well.
