Tafsir Tabari (تفسير الطبري)

Author: Imam Ibn Jarir Tabari (died 310 AH) محمد بن جریر طبری

Language: Arabic (العربية)

Tafsir Tabari is a classical Sunni Tafsir of the Holy Quran. Written by an early Persian scholar Imam Muhammad Ibn Jarir Tabari, this Tafsir was used by famous scholars like Ibn Kathir, Imam Suyuti and others in their own Tafsirs.

Many editions of this Tafsir available. One of the editions presented below is by Mahmud Muhammad Shakir, an Egyptian Arabic literateur famous for his research in early Islamic books. Another edition is by Dr. Abdullah Ibn Abdul Muhsin Turki.
