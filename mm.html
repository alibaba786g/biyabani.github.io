This research work is based on two very rare and ancient manuscripts of the first ever book of Hadith written by a Tabi'i scholar, narrated from Abu Hurairah. Although all these Hadiths are scattered in other Hadith books and

included in Musnad of Imam Ahmad bin Hanbal, here these are presented in the original form of a collection as written by Hammam bin Munabbih.

Research by Dr. Muhammad Hamidullah

Published in 2007

280 pages

Pages 94 to 112 were missing from the printed book.

Digitized by Maktabah Mujaddidiyah (www.maktabah.org) in September 2012
